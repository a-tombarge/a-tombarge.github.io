Conservation Easements in Rockbridge Co., VA

This map, hosted on CARTO, shows the conservation easements in Rockbridge County, Virginia. It also provides statistics about the easements overall and the individual easements when clicked. The data was obtained from VA DCR Natural Heritage.

Map of Lexington, Virginia

Flea Markets in the U.S.

This map was created as part of an extra challenge in MAP 371, and is my favorite from the course, showing the county with the highest rate of flea markets per person and the county with the largest number of flea markets. I created it in QGIS 2.18.10 with data obtained U.S. Census Bureau TIGER products and the POI Factory.

Amenities in Denver, CO

This map was created in MAP 672 using JavaScript. It reveals amenities located within 1 kilometer of any clickpoint in Denver. Amenities include stores with food, fire stations, and after-school programs.

About me

I've worked in land conservation for the past decade, assisting landowners seeking conservation easements with mapping and other services, including using GPS and photography to document current conditions of their properties. I hold a bachelor of science degree in geography with a concentration in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) from George Mason University.

I've recently started my own business with a partner and am working to master JavaScript, HTML, and CSS so that I can create more interesting paper and web maps for potential clients.
